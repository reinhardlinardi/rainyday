RainyDay

RainyDay is a news aggregator which provides real-time news search and news update. RainyDay was created by three young developers gathered to finish the last task of the 4th semester. They formed a team called "Rainy Day", which is a combination of our names : Rachel, Reinhard, and Wenny. The Great Britain has inspired them. It rains even in the summer. This depicts their working environment : they were always calm, even though there is constant tension coming from various deadlines.
